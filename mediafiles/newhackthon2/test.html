Make it personal

Some of our texts will feel spot-on, like we're watching your every move (we aren't). Others, at first glance, may seem like they don't pertain to you until you take a moment to think more deeply. But no matter which text you receive today, we encourage you to read it with an open mind.

When we text you, it's personal; you're the only person in the world to receive that text on that day at that time. Ask yourself:

Why do I need to hear this right now?
What does this have to do with me?
How can I apply this to my own life?
